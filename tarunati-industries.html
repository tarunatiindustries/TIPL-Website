Our Story

Built on a
Mission for
Clean Energy

Tarunati Industries was founded with a singular vision: harness the enormous agricultural biomass potential of Telangana and convert it into industrial-grade renewable fuel that powers India's factories cleanly.

Our Vision

To become India's leading biomass fuel company, enabling every industry to transition from coal to clean, renewable agricultural energy.

Our Mission

Manufacture premium biomass briquettes and pellets at consistent quality and scale, while creating rural livelihoods and reducing agricultural waste burning.

THE IDEA
Recognizing the Opportunity
Khammam district of Telangana — a biomass goldmine with saw dust, rice husk, cotton stalk, and palm residue abundantly available year-round from 200+ km radius.
FOUNDATION
Tarunati Industries Incorporated
Incorporated as a SC Women-Led Enterprise, registered under MSME, and recognized as a startup under DPIIT with a ₹76.9 Lakh initial project investment.
INFRASTRUCTURE
15,000 SFT Facility Operational
Complete manufacturing facility with briquette line, pellet line, industrial dryer, dust separator, and quality testing laboratory set up in Khammam.
SCALE-UP
5,000 Ton Annual Capacity
Full-scale production achieving 5,000 metric tons annual capacity with consistent 3800–4500 GCV fuel quality, serving industries across Telangana and beyond.
Why Khammam?

Khammam sits at the heart of Telangana's agricultural belt — surrounded by rice mills, saw mills, cotton fields, and palm plantations. This proximity to raw materials enables year-round uninterrupted production at lower logistics costs than any other location in the state.
